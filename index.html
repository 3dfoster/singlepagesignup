TrimWorks is an app being developed by a team of engineering students at UC Davis.

We've heard growers have a problem finding trustworthy, reliable workers to trim their cannabis for them, and we think we might have a solution.

TrimWorks is a web application that connects growers with safe, dependable trimmers.

It uses software to weed out lazy applicants, so growers only connect with trimmigrants who want to work.

We're collecting feedback from growers so we can develop our software to suit their needs.
